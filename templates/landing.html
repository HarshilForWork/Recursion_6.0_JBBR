How we work

Start by uploading a video

Simply paste a link to your Youtube video, or upload a video file, into Klap. Our rule of thumb: a one-minute long video produces about 5 video clips.

Let Trimly's AI magically create vertical videos for you

Just sit back and relax, while Trimly does all the work for you. In a matter of minutes, we will give you multiple viral-worthy clips from your original video that you can use Shorts.
